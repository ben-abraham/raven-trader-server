Raven-Trader!

  • What is?

    • Raven-Trader (RT) is a P2P (peer-to-peer) trading platform and exchange. It enables seamless one-step execution of transactions/trades on the ravencoin network.
    • Perform buy, sell, and trade orders directly with other users on the RavenCoin network, all while staying in complete ownership of all coins and assets you have.

  • Take money?

    • RT charges no comissions/fees for listing or executing trades. The only fees are ravencoin network fees, paid by the executing party.

  • Where coins?

    • You remain in control and retain direct ownership of all coins and assets at all points through a sale. Nothing is ever sent out of your wallet, until a transaction is executed.

  • How work?

    1. Party [A] Creates a buy/sell/trade order in Raven-Trader-Pro
    2. (Optional) Party [A] lists the trade on the RT website, either through app or manually.
    3. -- [A] Is 100% Done here, no need to even keep Raven-Trader-Pro open. --
    4. Party [B] Desires to purchase said asset.
    5. Party [B] retrieves [A]'s order, either through the website, or by direct communication with [A].
    6. Party [B] completes [A]'s order and published it to the network.
